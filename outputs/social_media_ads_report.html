Section 05
Key Takeaways & Recommendations
Five actionable findings distilled from the full EDA — ready to inform your next campaign strategy.
📣

Prioritise Instagram for ROI

Instagram consistently delivers the highest mean ROI across campaign types. Invest here first before diversifying to lower-cost channels.

🎯

Frame Campaigns as Product Launches

Product Launch goal campaigns outperform all others in ROI and conversion rate. Consider this framing even for retention or awareness objectives.

⏰

Schedule for Wednesday Mornings

Mid-week, mid-morning (9–11am) consistently produces peak engagement and ROI. Align campaign launches and boosted posts with this window.

💡

Use Pinterest for Budget Efficiency

Pinterest has the lowest acquisition cost in the dataset. For cost-sensitive campaigns, it offers a strong efficiency trade-off despite lower peak ROI.

📊

Track CTR — Not Just Impressions

High impressions alone are a vanity metric. CTR and Conversion Rate are much stronger predictors of campaign value and should guide optimisation decisions.

🚫

Don't Conflate Engagement with Conversion

Engagement Score and Conversion Rate may not be strongly correlated. Validate this in your own data — a high engagement score does not guarantee sales.

⭐ Sweet Spot Combination: Instagram + Product Launch goal + Wednesday 9–11am window — this trio consistently appears in the high-ROI, reasonable-cost quadrant across the analysis.
